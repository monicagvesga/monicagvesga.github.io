Orders & shipping

Where's my order?

Hi {first_name}, thanks for checking in. Your order {order_id} is on its way and currently {status}. You can follow it here: {tracking_link}, and it's expected by {date}. If it hasn't arrived by then, just reply to this message and I'll chase it for you right away.

Why it works: answers the question and the next one in one reply.

Orders & shipping

Shipping delay

Hi {first_name}, I want to be upfront with you: your order {order_id} is running late because of {reason}. I'm sorry, that's frustrating when you were expecting it. The new estimated date is {date}, and here's your tracking link: {tracking_link}. If that timing doesn't work, reply here and we'll set up a refund or a reshipment, your call.

Why it works: bad news first, clear ownership, a new date, and a way out.

Refunds

Refund approved

Hi {first_name}, good news: your refund of {amount} is approved and on its way back to your {payment_method} now. It usually lands within {time_frame}, and you'll get a confirmation email the moment it clears. Anything else I can help with while I'm here?

Why it works: confirms the money, sets timing, leaves the door open.

Refunds

Refund declined, kindly

Hi {first_name}, I wish I could say yes to this one. We're not able to refund {item} because {reason}. I know that's not the answer you were hoping for. What I can do instead is {alternative}, which gets you most of the way there. Want me to set that up?

Why it works: a warm no, a plain reason, and a real alternative.

Returns

Return or exchange

Hi {first_name}, happy to help you {return_or_exchange} this. Here's how it works: {step_1}, then {step_2}, then {step_3}. I've emailed you a prepaid label, so you won't pay for shipping. Once it reaches us, your {refund_or_replacement} goes out within {time_frame}. Just let me know if anything's unclear.

Why it works: turns a chore into three steps and removes the cost worry.

Tricky moments

De-escalation opener

Hi {first_name}, you're right to be frustrated, and I'm sorry this has landed on you. Let me get straight to it: {what_you_are_doing_now}. Give me {time} and I'll come back with a real fix, not a holding message. I've got this from here.

Why it works: validates the feeling, takes ownership, promises one next step.

Tricky moments

I don't have the answer yet

Hi {first_name}, thanks for your patience. I don't want to give you a half-answer, so I'm checking the details with our {team} team now. I'll come back to you by {time} with a clear update. You don't need to do anything in the meantime.

Why it works: honest about the wait, sets a deadline, no effort for them.

Subscriptions

Pause or cancel a subscription

Hi {first_name}, no problem at all, I can {pause_or_cancel} your subscription. Before I do, would {alternative} work better for you? Either way is completely fine, just tell me which and it's done. No hoops.

Why it works: offers a softer option without pushing, and makes leaving easy.

Tricky moments

A sensitive or personal question

Hi {first_name}, thanks for asking, and please don't feel awkward, this is exactly the kind of thing we're here for. {clear_answer}. If anything is still unclear, just tell me and we'll keep going until it's solved. Whatever it is, we'll keep it simple and discreet.

Why it works: normalizes the question, answers plainly, reassures on privacy.

Closing

A friendly close

Hi {first_name}, glad we got that taken care of. I'll leave this open for a couple of days in case anything else comes up, so feel free to reply if it does. Take care, and thanks for your patience today.

Why it works: a warm goodbye that invites them back without nagging.

Why it matters

One reply beats five

A good macro turns a back-and-forth into a one-touch resolution, what most help desks count as first-contact resolution (FCR).

Fewer back-and-forths for the customer, a faster queue for the team.
The takeaway. A template is not about sounding scripted. Done right, it lets you be fast and human at the same time, and it frees you to spend real attention on the cases that actually need it.
